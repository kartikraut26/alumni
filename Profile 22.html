Sonali Bendre (Born: 1 January 1975

          is an Indian actress primarily known for her work in Hindi films along with a few Telugu and Tamil films. She did modelling assignments prior to making her acting debut with Aag (1994), which won her the Filmfare Award for New Face of the Year and Screen Award for Best Female Debut. She completed her schooling from Kendriya Vidyalaya, Bangalore and completed her graduation from Ramnarain Ruia College, Mumbai.
