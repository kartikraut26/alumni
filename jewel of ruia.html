Ruia College Alumni Association (RCAA)

         Alumni always have a significant role in any College as they build the image of the institution to which they belong, in the real world. In fact, alumni are the institution’s reflection in the society’s mirror.
Since the year 2006, Ruia College has been felicitating its illustrious alumni with the 'Jewel of Ruia' awards held in the latter half of the academic year. This is a special award given to the alumni in recognition of their contribution in respective areas of specialisation. The Institute is proud of its Alumni and their success in the professional career. The Ruia College alumni have always contributed in the development of the College in various ways.
