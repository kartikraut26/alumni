Arun Shankarrao Sarnaik(Born: 04 october 1935)

          was an Indian actor and singer from Kolhapur, Maharashtra. He was the son of the famous singer "Maharashtrakokil" Pt. Shankarao Sarnaik and brother of famous classical singer "Pandit Nivruttibua Sarnaik" from Jaipur Atrauli Gharana . Arun Sarnaik completed his graduation from Ruia College in Mumbai.
