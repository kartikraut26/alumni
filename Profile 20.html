Shruti Sadolikar Katkar (Born: 9 November 1951)

          is an Indian classical singer of the khyal style in the Jaipur-Atrauli gharanaShe is a recipient of the Sangeet Natak Akademi Award for Hindustani vocal music for 2011 Sadolikar was born 1951 into a family from Kolhapur which was known for its musical tradition. She received training in Indian classical music from childhood. Sadolikar performs all forms of Indian classical and semi-classical music, including thumri, tappa, and natya sangeet, and has performed in India, Canada, the United States, France, Switzerland, Germany, and the West Asian countries.
